Stand guard over your heart
Joshua 23: Now, vigilantly guard your souls: Love God, your God.
The final pages of the book of Joshua contain the sermon Joshua preached to the people of Israel in his old age. It‚Äôs a mere “sermonette” when compared Moses’ farewell that fills most of the book of Deuteronomy.
