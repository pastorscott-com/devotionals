It wasn’t that I now understood all there was to know, but I grasped this promise of God to me. If he called me to the ministry, he would help me do ministry. Over 40 years later, I can report that the Lord has been faithful to keep that promise.
Take Away: If the Lord calls you to it he will help you do it.

1Peter 2: Treat everyone with dignity. Love your spiritual family. Revere God. Respect the government.

Devotional on Joshua

November 30, 2022 GR 'Scott' Cundiff


Make a pile of stones

It‚Äôs a story of God’s grace, patience, and mercy. It‚Äôs the story of his unfailing love. Here’s a tip right from God’s Word: build some memorials in your life.
