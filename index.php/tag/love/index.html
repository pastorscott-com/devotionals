Devotional on Ecclesiastes

God, enjoying life with me
Ecclesiastes 9: God takes pleasure in your pleasure!
I know that a common view of God is that he‚Äôs against our enjoying life and that his favorite word is “no!” That is very mistaken. It‚Äôs true that God has a lot of “no’s” for us.
